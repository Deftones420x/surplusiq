A Little Bit About Us

Company Details

REALAUCTION (Realauction.com) provides an array of online software solutions including: delinquent Tax Lien/Tax Certificate, Foreclosure, Sheriff Sales, Land Sales, and other municipal property auctions. REALAUCTION develops and hosts all our client sites, allowing bidders and investors to participate online from the comfort of their own homes or offices.
Founded in 2004, we have conducted over 1.5M online auctions to date.
In 2008, REALAUCTION conducted the first ever online judicial foreclosure auction in the United States. Today, we have become the largest online foreclosure software provider in the country. Our software solutions help municipality and county governments across the country reduce the costs and manpower associated with traditional live auctions by conducting sales via the Internet in compliance with state statutes.

Our Commitment to our Customers

In an increasingly competitive world, REALAUCTION provides cost-effective, innovative online auction solutions that help municipal, county and state government agencies reach their full operating potential.

We are a trusted partner and work cooperatively with our clients to mitigate their toughest foreclosure, delinquent tax lien and tax deed management auction challenges. We offer user friendly software, a dedicated customer service department, & free online training. Our clients rely on us to assist in improving their processes & reducing the operating costs of holding live auctions.
